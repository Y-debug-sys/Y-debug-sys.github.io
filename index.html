Xinyu Yuan

Graduate Student, Hefei University of Technology
Email: yxy5315@gmail.com

I am a graduate student at School of Computer Science and Information Engineering at HFUT, advised by Assoc. Prof. Yan Qiao. And I received my B.Eng. from the School of Information and Artificial Intelligence at Anhui Agricultual University.

My primary research interests includes network management, machine learning, AI and network measurement based on Sketch. I am actively working on real-world network traffic monitoring with machine learning. I am also working on studying of the powerful generative models (such as diffusion models and LLMs).
